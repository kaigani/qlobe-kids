# Unicorn (Starlight) — puppet rig character sheet

Plush rainbow unicorn, rigged for the segmented spline-puppet engine
(`shared/js/stage/puppet.js`). Test/tune in `puppet-studio.html?char=unicorn`.

## Parts
`parts/*.png` — 10 body parts sliced from
`00-reference/puppet parts/unicorn/sprites-unicorn.png` (a 3×3 part grid,
background removed via the local `qwen-image-layered` workflow). Head, torso,
arm-upper/arm-lower (+paw) ×2, leg-upper/leg-lower (+hoof) ×2.

## Lip-sync — viseme head swap
`anim/head-{a,o,e,wr,ts,ln,uq,mbp,fv}.png` (512², transparent). Source grid
`unicorn/head-visemes.png` → sliced to 9 grey tiles → `qwen-image-layered`
extraction → **re-registered by the white face** (the flowing mane redrew at
varying scale; aligning the face tightened the set from ~60px spread to ~2px so
the swap doesn't jitter) → downsized to 512². Head part fitted to the body:
`anchor [0.5, 0.778], scale 0.760`. Rest = `ts`.

## Voice
`voice/intro.m4a` + `voice/intro.cues.json` — "Greetings, I am Starlight and I
love making wishes come true." Segmented from `fox/concept-video-2.mp4`; cues via
`tools/lipsync/whisper-visemes.py` (values are viseme names → `VISEME_IDENTITY`).
Listed in the `VOICES` map in `puppet-studio.html`.
