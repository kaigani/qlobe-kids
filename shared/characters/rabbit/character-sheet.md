# Rabbit (Rocky) — puppet rig character sheet

Plush long-eared rabbit, rigged for the segmented spline-puppet engine. Test/tune
in `puppet-studio.html?char=rabbit`.

## Parts
`parts/*.png` — 10 body parts sliced from
`00-reference/puppet parts/rabbit/sprites-rabbit.png` (qwen-layered transparent).
Head (long ears), torso, arm-upper/arm-lower (+paw) ×2, leg-upper/leg-lower
(+foot) ×2.

## Lip-sync — viseme head swap
`anim/head-{a,o,e,wr,ts,ln,uq,mbp,fv}.png` (512², transparent). Source grid
`rabbit/head-visemes-long-ears.png` → sliced NON-DESTRUCTIVELY with
`tools/lipsync/slice-viseme-grid.py` (bg → #919191, art untouched) → extracted via
`qwen-image-layered` (all 9 clean on seed 42, ~69% transp) → **re-registered by
width + chin** (the long ears vary above the aligned face; aligning by the chin
keeps the mouth stable — width spread 4px, chin 3px) → downsized to 512². Head part
fitted `anchor [0.5, 0.834], scale 0.878`. Rest = `ts`.

## Voice
`voice/intro.m4a` + `voice/intro.cues.json` — "My name is Rocky Rabbit. Happy to
meet you!" Segmented from the first `concept-video.mp4`; cues via
`tools/lipsync/whisper-visemes.py`. In the `VOICES` map in `puppet-studio.html`.

## Notes / next

  (`shared/characters/default-rig.json`) — new rigs start here, then nudge to fit.
