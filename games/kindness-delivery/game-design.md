### Mini-GDD: Kindness Delivery (games/kindness-delivery/)

**Category:** social-emotional · **World:** story-screen · **Engine:**
observe-journal · **splashEmoji:** 💌
**Premise:** run a kindness post office! Each page presents someone who could
use a smile (a tired grown-up, a friend with a scraped knee, a new neighbor);
the child chooses what to make and "delivers" it — then is invited to make the
REAL thing off-tablet. Every delivery stamps a heart in the kindness journal.
**Learning:** empathy, noticing others' feelings, generosity as a habit,
connecting play to real acts.
**Modes:**
1. `deliveries` "Make a Delivery" (3 pages): recipients — tired grown-up
   (emoji:🥱), sad friend (emoji:🩹), new neighbor (emoji:🏠); deliverables
   per page: drawing 🖍️, note 💌, flower 🌼, helping hands 🤝.
2. `secret` "Secret Kindness" (2 pages): do something kind without being seen
   (tidy a toy 🧸, save the last cookie 🍪) — the sneakiest smiles.
**Voice tone:** warm conspiratorial ("Let's make someone's WHOLE day");
always end pages with the real-world nudge.
**Assets needed:** art — recipient portraits, deliverable stickers, postal
satchel; voice — all config lines.
