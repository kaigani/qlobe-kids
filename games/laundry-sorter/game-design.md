### Mini-GDD: Laundry Sorter (games/laundry-sorter/)
**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** sort-into-bins · **splashEmoji:** 🧦
**Premise:** sort the laundry! Socks and shirts by color into baskets. Movement
prompt in the cheer: "Can you help sort real socks today?"
**Learning:** color sorting, categorizing, helping-at-home pride.
**Modes:** 1. `colors` "Sort by Color" (rounds 3, itemsPerRound 4): bins = red
basket (swatch:#e23d3d) / blue basket (swatch:#2d7dd2); items = red things
(🧦 say "a red sock!", 👕 red shirt, 🧣 scarf) vs blue (🧢 cap, 🧤 mitten, 👖
jeans) — emoji + say carries color when glyph color is ambiguous; note in ASSETS.
2. `kinds` "Socks and Shirts" (rounds 3): bins 🧦 vs 👕; items mixed clothing.
**Assets needed:** colored clothing card art; voice lines.
