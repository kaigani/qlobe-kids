### Mini-GDD: Snack Chef (games/snack-chef/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🥪
**Premise:** a real kitchen coaching session — the child makes an actual snack
with a grown-up nearby while the tablet walks the steps slowly: wash hands,
peel, spread, slice (butter knife!), plate, and clean up. Cooking IS the game.
**Learning:** kitchen sequencing, safe tool handling, independence, cleanup as
part of cooking.
**Modes:**
1. `banana` "Banana Coins" (type steps): 1 wash hands, 20s (emoji:🧼) →
   2 peel the banana (emoji:🍌) → 3 slice coins with a butter knife, 45s
   (emoji:🔪, say grown-up watches, fingers curled like a claw) →
   4 arrange coins on the plate (emoji:🍽️) → 5 wipe the counter (emoji:🧻).
2. `toast` "Spread It" (type steps): 1 wash hands (emoji:🧼) → 2 spread soft
   butter or jam corner-to-corner, 40s (emoji:🍞) → 3 cut into triangles
   (emoji:🔺) → 4 taste test! (emoji:😋) → 5 dishes to the sink (emoji:🧽).
**Voice tone:** calm sous-chef; safety phrases woven in naturally, never
scary; celebrate the eating at the end.
**Assets needed:** art — banana, toast, butter knife, plate, cutting board;
voice — all config lines.
