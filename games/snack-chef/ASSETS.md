# Snack Chef Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used. The Storybook Rooms background at `assets/bg.jpg` is intentionally not included; the reviewer supplies it.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Banana: a ripe banana shown whole, peeled, and sliced into coins.
- Toast: one friendly slice suitable for a child-sized preparation scene.
- Butter knife: a clearly blunt, kid-safe butter knife.
- Plate: a simple plate holding neatly arranged banana coins or toast.
- Cutting board: a stable board for slicing with a grown-up nearby.

Voice lines:

- Welcome, sous-chef! A grown-up stays nearby while we make our snack slowly and safely.
- Nicely done, sous-chef.
- Snack complete! Celebrate with a delicious taste.
- Banana coins are ready to eat. Bon appétit, chef!
- Wash your hands with soap for twenty seconds.
- Clean hands are ready to cook.
- Peel the banana.
- Your grown-up watches. Curl your fingers like a claw, then slice banana coins with a butter knife.
- Slow slices and curled fingers. Safe chef work!
- Arrange the banana coins on the plate.
- Wipe the counter. Cleanup is part of cooking.
- Your toast is ready. Time for the chef taste test!
- Wash your hands with soap.
- Spread soft butter or jam slowly, all the way from corner to corner.
- Corner to corner. That is careful spreading!
- With your grown-up nearby, cut the toast into triangles with a butter knife.
- Taste test! Take a bite and enjoy what you made.
- Carry the dishes to the sink. Cleanup completes the recipe.
