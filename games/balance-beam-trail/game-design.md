### Mini-GDD: Balance Beam Trail (games/balance-beam-trail/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **World:** field-journal · **Engine:**
coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🤸
**Premise:** a floor line, a curb, a low log — anything becomes a balance
trail. The tablet coaches heel-to-toe walking, arms-out airplane, backwards
steps, and the wobbly-but-recovering superpower ("wobbles mean your balance
is LEARNING").
**Learning:** balance, body control, fall-safe confidence, focus while
moving.
**Modes:**
1. `floor` "Tape Trail" (type steps): 1 make a tape or chalk line (emoji:📏)
   → 2 heel-to-toe walk it slowly, 45s (emoji:🦶) → 3 airplane arms pass,
   45s (emoji:✈️) → 4 backwards pass, tiny steps, 60s (emoji:↩️) →
   5 superstar finish pose (emoji:🌟).
2. `outdoor` "Log and Curb" (type steps): with a grown-up spotter — 1 find a
   low log or curb (emoji:🪵) → 2 spotter hand-hold pass, 60s (emoji:🤝) →
   3 solo slow pass, 60s (emoji:🧘) → 4 stop in the MIDDLE and balance for
   five breaths (emoji:⏸️) → 5 hop-off dismount and ta-da! (emoji:🎉).
**Voice tone:** gymnastics coach calm; wobble-positive language throughout.
**Assets needed:** art — tape line, log, airplane-arms figure; voice — all
config lines.
