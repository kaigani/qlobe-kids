### Mini-GDD: Picture Narration (games/picture-narration/)

**Category:** oral-storytelling · **Engine:** observe-journal · **splashEmoji:** 🖼️
**Premise:** one big busy picture per round (a market, a park, a harbor). The
teacher voice asks "what do you see?" and the child stamps stickers on every
thing they can NAME out loud — naming is the game. The recap replays their
noticing as a told story.
**Learning:** descriptive vocabulary, observation, speaking in sentences,
detail hunting.
**Modes:**
1. `park` "At the Park" (3 pages): park scene (emoji:🏞️) with stickers like
   🐕 🪁 🌳 🦆 ⛲; prompts "Find something that flies. Say what it is!"
2. `market` "At the Market" (3 pages): market scene (emoji:🧺) with 🍎 🥕 🧀
   🌽 🐓.
**Voice tone:** curious co-explorer; repeat the child's likely words back
("A kite! It dances in the wind!").
**Assets needed:** art — three busy full-scene illustrations per mode subject,
sticker art; voice — all config lines.
