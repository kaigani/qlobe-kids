### Mini-GDD: Red Light, Green Light (games/red-green-light/)

**Category:** social-emotional · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🚦
**Premise:** the tablet IS the traffic light. Kid (and family!) move on green,
freeze on red — impulse control as a giggling full-body game. Prop the tablet up
and play across the room.
**Learning:** impulse control, stop/go self-regulation, listening while moving.
**Modes:**
1. `classic` (type signal, rounds 5): states go (emoji:🟢, '#58a945', pop,
   say "Green light! Go go go!", 3–8s) and stop (emoji:🔴, '#c9503a', boing,
   say "Red light! Freeze like a statue!", 2–5s).
2. `silly` (type signal, rounds 5): adds a third state wiggle (emoji:🟡,
   '#f4c53d', silly sfx, say "Yellow light! Wiggle in place!", 2–4s) between
   go and stop — harder switching, more giggles.
**Voice tone:** energetic, playful, big contrast between GO excitement and FREEZE
drama.
**Assets needed:** art — friendly traffic light character (future mascot candidate),
go/stop/wiggle poses; voice — all config lines.
