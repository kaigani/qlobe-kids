# Red Light, Green Light Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Friendly traffic light character: warm traffic-light mascot with clear red, yellow, and green states.
- Go pose: kid-friendly movement pose that reads as energetic forward motion.
- Stop pose: statue-like frozen pose with playful drama.
- Wiggle pose: in-place silly movement pose for the yellow-light cue.

Voice lines:

- Watch the light and listen.
- Great switching.
- You stopped and went with your whole body!
- You listened and froze like a statue!
- Green light! Go go go!
- Red light! Freeze like a statue!
- You switched your body in silly ways!
- Yellow light! Wiggle in place!
