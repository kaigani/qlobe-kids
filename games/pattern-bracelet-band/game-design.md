### Mini-GDD: Pattern Bracelet Band (games/pattern-bracelet-band/)
**Category:** art-music · **Engine:** pattern-continue · **splashEmoji:** 📿
**Premise:** string a bead bracelet by continuing its pattern. Each solved round
closes the bracelet with a sparkle and connects screen work to real craft play.
**Learning:** patterning as art/craft, color sequences.
**Modes:** 1. `beads` "String the Beads" (rounds 5, difficultyRamp): red, yellow,
blue, and purple bead swatches in AB, ABC, and ABB patterns, with one- and
two-bead endings.
**Assets needed:** glossy bead art; recorded voice lines.
