# Shape-to-Picture Challenge - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Shape-to-Picture Challenge (games/shape-to-picture/)
**Category:** art-music · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🖼️
**Premise:** turn plain shapes into a picture - circles become a caterpillar,
triangles become a sailboat. The ghost outline reveals what the shapes will
become; the say line names the surprise.
**Learning:** visual imagination, shape recombination (art from geometry).
**Modes:** 1. `pictures` "Shape Pictures" (rounds 4): caterpillar, sailboat,
ice cream, and snowman from emoji shape pieces. 2. `faces` "Shape Faces"
(rounds 3): robot face, cat face, and silly monster from mixed shapes.
**Assets needed:** glossy shape pieces; reveal-art per picture; voice lines.
