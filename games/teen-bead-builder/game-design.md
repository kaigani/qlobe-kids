# Teen Bead Builder - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Teen Bead Builder (games/teen-bead-builder/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🔟
**Premise:** Montessori teen boards: a ten bar plus some ones makes a teen number. Kids place golden bead placeholders and the numeral card, then hear the completed ten-plus-ones sentence.
**Learning:** teens as ten-plus-ones, a first place-value seed.
**Modes:** 1. `teens` "Build a Teen" (rounds 5): builds for 11, 13, 15, 12, 14 using one ten-bar placeholder, N unit bead placeholders, and a numeral card.
**Assets needed:** golden bead-bar art; recorded count-on voice lines.
