### Mini-GDD: Animal Walk Cards (games/animal-walk-cards/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **World:** field-journal · **Engine:**
coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🐻
**Premise:** the classic animal-walk deck as a coached routine: bear crawl,
frog jump, crab walk, flamingo stand — each with a timed round, animal-sound
moments, and a final "animal parade" chaining them all.
**Learning:** gross-motor strength and coordination, body-shape imitation,
cardio play, movement memory.
**Modes:**
1. `learn` "Animal School" (type steps): 1 bear crawl — hands and feet, bottom
   up, 30s (emoji:🐻) → 2 frog jump — crouch low, LEAP, 30s (emoji:🐸) →
   3 crab walk — belly to the sky, 30s (emoji:🦀) → 4 flamingo — one leg,
   count five flamingo breaths (emoji:🦩) → 5 cool-down turtle stretch
   (emoji:🐢).
2. `parade` "Animal Parade" (type steps): chain all four walks back-to-back,
   20s each with sound effects — roar, ribbit, click-click, flamingo hum —
   then invite the family to join the parade line (emoji:🎪).
**Voice tone:** zookeeper playful; every animal gets its sound moment.
**Assets needed:** art — animal pose cards (bear, frog, crab, flamingo,
turtle); voice — all config lines.
