### Mini-GDD: Shadow Chase (games/shadow-chase/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **World:** field-journal · **Engine:**
observe-journal · **splashEmoji:** 🌤️
**Premise:** a sunny-day science walk about SHADOWS. Each page invites a real
observation ("Stand outside. Where is YOUR shadow?"), then the child records
what they saw with stickers — long shadow, short shadow, shadow hiding. The
journal becomes their shadow diary across a day.
**Learning:** sun/shadow cause-and-effect, time-of-day awareness, observation
outdoors, body movement (chase your shadow, shrink it, make shadow shapes).
**Modes:**
1. `morning` "Morning Shadows" (3 pages): pages for find-your-shadow
   (emoji:🧍), make-a-shadow-shape (emoji:🤸), shadow-of-a-thing (emoji:🌳);
   stickers: long 📏, short 🤏, wiggly 〰️, hiding 🙈.
2. `day` "All-Day Watch" (3 pages): same shadow checked morning ☀️, noon 🕛,
   evening 🌇 — say what changed.
**Voice tone:** playful field scientist; every page sends the child MOVING.
**Assets needed:** art — child-with-shadow illustrations at different sun
angles, shadow-shape stickers; voice — all config lines.
