### Mini-GDD: Rhythm Copycat (games/rhythm-copycat/)
**Category:** art-music · **Engine:** pattern-continue · **splashEmoji:** 👏
**Premise:** body-percussion patterns. Clap-stomp-clap-stomp; the child taps what
comes next, then does the pattern with their body.
**Learning:** rhythm patterns, body percussion, audiation.
**Modes:** 1. `copycat` "Clap and Stomp" (rounds 5, difficultyRamp): clap, stomp,
and pat actions in AB and ABC patterns. 2. `animals`
"Animal Beats" (rounds 4): frog, bird, and lion beat patterns using playful
spoken animal sounds.
**Assets needed:** action pose art; recorded percussion-word lines; future real
clap/stomp sounds.
