# Neighborhood Map Walk Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used. The Field Journal background at `./assets/bg.jpg` is a declared production-art slot and is not included in this stub.
