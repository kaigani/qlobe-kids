# Clay Creature Studio - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Clay Creature Studio (games/clay-creature-studio/)
**Category:** art-music · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🐲
**Premise:** sculpt a creature from clay parts - body, googly eyes, horns, feet,
wings, and spikes - onto the ghost. The finished creature roars or squeaks its
own say line. The final cheer invites real clay: "Squish a real one later!"
**Learning:** part-whole construction, creative craft confidence.
**Modes:** 1. `creatures` "Build a Creature" (rounds 4): monster, dragon,
caterpillar, and bird. 2. `silly` "Mix-Up Creatures" (rounds 3): mismatched
creatures including dragon-duck, monster-bird, and caterpillar-dragon.
**Assets needed:** clay-textured part art; creature voice lines.
