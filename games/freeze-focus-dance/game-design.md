### Mini-GDD: Freeze Focus Dance (games/freeze-focus-dance/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **World:** paper-garden · **Engine:**
coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🕺
**Premise:** freeze dance with a focus twist: dance WILD on the music
signal, freeze SOLID on the stop — and while frozen, the tablet gives a tiny
focus mission ("keep still AND look at something blue"). Stillness becomes
as fun as the wiggles.
**Learning:** impulse control, body-stop regulation, attention while still,
joyful energy release.
**Modes:**
1. `classic` "Dance and Freeze" (type signal, rounds 6): states dance
   (emoji:🕺, '#f08a24', pop, say "DANCE! Shake everything!", 5–10s) and
   freeze (emoji:🧊, '#2d7dd2', boing, say "FREEZE! Solid as ice!", 3–5s).
2. `focus` "Freeze Missions" (type signal, rounds 6): dance state plus
   mission-freezes — freeze-and-find-something-blue (emoji:🔵), freeze-on-
   one-foot (emoji:🦶), freeze-with-a-silly-face (emoji:🤪), freeze-and-
   breathe-quiet (emoji:😮‍💨).
**Voice tone:** DJ meets mindfulness teacher; the freeze is the punchline.
**Assets needed:** art — dance/freeze state cards, mission icons; voice —
all config lines.
