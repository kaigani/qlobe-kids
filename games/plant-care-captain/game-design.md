### Mini-GDD: Plant Care Captain (games/plant-care-captain/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🪴
**Premise:** the child is captain of a real plant. The tablet runs a gentle
care routine — look closely, feel the soil, water slowly, mist the leaves,
and log how the plant is doing today. Repeatable daily; observation is the
heart of it.
**Learning:** living-thing care, gentle handling, routine responsibility,
close observation.
**Modes:**
1. `care` "Care Round" (type steps): 1 say good morning to your plant
   (emoji:🪴) → 2 look closely: any new leaves? 30s (emoji:🔍) → 3 finger in
   the soil — wet or dry? (emoji:👆) → 4 water slooowly if dry, 30s
   (emoji:💧, say stop when the soil drinks it up) → 5 turn the pot toward
   the sun (emoji:☀️).
2. `spa` "Plant Spa Day" (type steps): 1 dust one leaf gently with a soft
   cloth, 40s (emoji:🍃) → 2 mist around the leaves, 20s (emoji:💦) →
   3 trim one brown bit with a grown-up (emoji:✂️) → 4 tell your plant it
   looks great (emoji:💚).
**Voice tone:** ship-captain warm; the plant is crew.
**Assets needed:** art — potted plant with moods, watering can, mister,
soft cloth; voice — all config lines.
