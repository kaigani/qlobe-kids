# Shape Tangram Tales — Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Shape Tangram Tales (games/tangram-tales/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🦊
**Premise:** build animals from shapes on their ghost outlines; the built animal
"comes to life" in the say line ("Your fox says hello!").
**Learning:** shape composition, spatial reasoning, part-whole thinking.
**Modes:** 1. `animals` "Build the Animal" (rounds 4): fox (triangles 🔺 +
square 🟧 arrangement), cat, boat, tree — each 4-6 emoji-shape parts (🔺🟨🟩🟦🟪
mix) with clear ghost positions. 2. `things` "Build the Thing" (rounds 3):
rocket, house, flower.
**Assets needed:** proper tangram-piece art (flat glossy shapes); voice lines.
