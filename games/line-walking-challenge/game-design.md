### Mini-GDD: Line Walking Challenge (games/line-walking-challenge/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🧘
**Premise:** the classic Montessori walking-on-the-line, coached with love:
slow heel-to-toe walking along a floor line — then with a bell that must not
ring, then with a beanbag on the head, then carrying a (plastic!) cup of
water. Slowness is the whole skill.
**Learning:** concentration, balance, graceful controlled movement,
self-calming through slow work.
**Modes:**
1. `line` "The Quiet Line" (type steps): 1 make an ellipse or long line with
   tape (emoji:➰) → 2 walk it heel-to-toe, slow as a snail, 60s (emoji:🐌)
   → 3 again with hands behind your back, 60s (emoji:🧍) → 4 stop halfway,
   close eyes, three breaths (emoji:😌) → 5 finish and bow (emoji:🙇).
2. `carry` "Carry Challenge" (type steps): walk the line with — a beanbag on
   your head, 60s (emoji:🧢) → a bell that must stay silent, 60s (emoji:🔔)
   → a cup of water, 90s (emoji:🥤, plastic cup, tiny splash = fine) → the
   grand combo: beanbag AND cup (emoji:🎩).
**Voice tone:** near-whisper serenity; the slowest walker wins at life.
**Assets needed:** art — floor ellipse, beanbag, bell, cup; voice — all
config lines.
