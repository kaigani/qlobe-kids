# Line Walking Challenge Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. The Field Journal background is referenced at `./assets/bg.jpg` but is intentionally not included; the reviewer supplies it. No generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Floor ellipse: a clear taped ellipse or long floor line in the Field Journal art world.
- Beanbag: a soft child-safe beanbag suitable for balancing on the head.
- Bell: a small handheld bell used for a silent carrying challenge.
- Cup: a child-safe plastic cup holding only a little water.

Voice lines:

- Let's move as slowly and quietly as a snail.
- So slow and steady. Beautiful work.
- You walked with calm, careful balance!
- You finished the quiet line with grace!
- Ask a grown-up to make an ellipse or a long line with tape.
- Walk heel to toe, as slowly as a snail. Slowness is the skill.
- The snail says, that was wonderfully slow.
- Walk the line again with your hands behind your back.
- Your careful feet stayed in charge.
- Stop halfway. Close your eyes and take three slow breaths.
- Finish the line slowly, then take a graceful bow.
- You carried everything with calm, careful balance!
- Balance a beanbag on your head and walk the line very slowly.
- Your head was a calm little beanbag shelf.
- Carry a bell along the line. Move so gently that it stays silent.
- What quiet, careful carrying.
- Carry a plastic cup with a little water. A tiny splash is just fine.
- Careful beats fast, and you moved with care.
- Grand combo! Balance the beanbag and carry the cup along the line.
