### Mini-GDD: Magnet Explorer (games/magnet-explorer/)
**Category:** sensorial-science · **Engine:** sort-into-bins · **splashEmoji:** 🧲
**Premise:** which things stick to a magnet? Sort into "sticks!" (🧲) and "doesn't
stick" (🧺). The voice explains after each: "A paperclip is metal — it sticks!"
**Learning:** magnetic vs non-magnetic, early materials science.
**Modes:** 1. `explore` "Sticks or Not?" (rounds 3, itemsPerRound 4): magnetic —
paperclip 📎, key 🔑, nail? use 🔩, scissors ✂️, spoon 🥄; not — leaf 🍃, crayon
🖍️, teddy 🧸, cup? plastic duck 🐤, cork? 🪵 wood block. Say lines teach WHY.
**Assets needed:** object card art; voice lines with the explanations.
