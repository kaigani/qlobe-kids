### Mini-GDD: Emotion Voice Game (games/emotion-voice-game/)

**Category:** oral-storytelling · **Engine:** observe-journal · **splashEmoji:** 🎭
**Premise:** say the same little line in different feelings! Each round shows
a sentence scene ("We are going to the moon!") and emotion stickers; the child
picks a feeling, HEARS the coach model it, then performs the line that way and
stamps it into their feelings journal.
**Learning:** emotional expression and recognition, prosody, performing
confidence.
**Modes:**
1. `lines` "Say It With Feeling" (3 pages): lines like "We are going to the
   moon!" (emoji:🚀), "Where did my shoe go?" (emoji:👟), "Dinner is ready!"
   (emoji:🍝); feelings per page: happy 😊, scared 😨, proud 🥳, sleepy 😴.
2. `animals` "Animal Feelings" (2 pages): say "hello" as a grumpy bear (🐻),
   an excited puppy (🐶), a shy mouse (🐭).
**Voice tone:** theatrical coach — the voice itself models each emotion
broadly so the child can copy it.
**Assets needed:** art — emotion-face stickers, scene cards; voice — all
config lines INCLUDING one modeled performance per emotion per line.
