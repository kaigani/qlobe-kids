# Blend Train — Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Blend Train (games/blend-train/)
**Category:** reading-phonics · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🚂
**Premise:** the original queue star — push sound cars together to build a word!
The onset car and rime car couple up on the track; when the build completes the
voice blends slowly then says the word ("mmm… aaat… MAT!").
**Learning:** onset-rime blending (the Sound Sprouts skill, drag-to-build form).
**Modes:** 1. `couple` "Couple the Cars" (rounds 5, ordered): each build = onset
tile then rime tile side by side (parts use shared:letter-tiles refs; e.g. m+at,
c+at, s+un, d+og, p+ig — verify tiles exist), say = the blend line. 2. `three`
"Three-Car Trains" (rounds 4): onset + rime + a caboose word-card
(text:<word>) placed last as the "whole word" car.
**Assets needed:** train-car frames wrapping the letter tiles; blend voice lines.
