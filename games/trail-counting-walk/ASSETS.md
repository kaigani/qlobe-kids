# Trail Counting Walk Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. The Field Journal background is referenced at `./assets/bg.jpg` but is intentionally not included; the reviewer supplies it. No generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Counting tokens: bold, friendly tokens that can represent collected outdoor totals.
- Stair icon: a clear outdoor stair or trail-step subject.
- Bird icon: a friendly bird shown both watching and listening.
- Door icon: a bright red door that makes the color rule obvious.

Voice lines:

- This is the counting desk. Today, every number is big news!
- Number collected! That is today's latest headline.
- Breaking news! You counted your whole walk!
- Breaking news! Your counting walk has a grand total!
- First mission: count every stair or step. Remember your number for check-in.
- Counting desk check-in! Say your stair or step number out loud.
- Bird report! Count every bird you see or hear. Remember your number.
- Bird desk check-in! Say your bird number out loud.
- Color mission! Count only red doors. Other colors do not join this total.
- Red door check-in! Announce your red door number.
- You choose the headline. Pick one thing to count and remember your number.
- Reporter check-in! Tell us what you counted and your number.
- Grand total ceremony! Say all your numbers, biggest first.
- Target counted! You found the number.
- Headline: every number target has been found!
- Find and count five round things.
- Find and count four growing things.
- Find and count three things with wheels.
- Find and count two things that might be older than grandma.
- Find one thing that makes you smile.
