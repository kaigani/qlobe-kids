### Mini-GDD: Trail Counting Walk (games/trail-counting-walk/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🔢
**Premise:** a walk where EVERYTHING gets counted: stairs climbed, birds
spotted, red doors passed, pinecones pocketed. The tablet sets counting
missions and collects the numbers at each check-in — ending with the day's
grand total, announced like breaking news.
**Learning:** counting in the wild, number memory, comparison
(more/fewer), observation stamina.
**Modes:**
1. `walk` "Counting Walk" (type steps): 1 count every stair or step for the
   first stretch, 2min (emoji:🪜) → 2 bird count — every bird seen OR heard,
   2min (emoji:🐦) → 3 door-color mission: count only RED doors, 2min
   (emoji:🚪) → 4 pick your own thing to count, 2min (emoji:🎯) → 5 grand
   total ceremony: say all your numbers, biggest first (emoji:📊).
2. `race` "Number Race" (type steps): find and count to beat targets — 5
   round things (emoji:⚪), 4 growing things (emoji:🌱), 3 things with
   wheels (emoji:🛞), 2 things older than grandma maybe (emoji:🏛️), 1 thing
   that makes you smile (emoji:😊).
**Voice tone:** number-newsroom excitement; every total is a headline.
**Assets needed:** art — counting tokens, stair/bird/door icons; voice — all
config lines.
