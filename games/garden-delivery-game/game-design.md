### Mini-GDD: Garden Delivery Game (games/garden-delivery-game/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🚚
**Premise:** the child runs a garden delivery service: carry water across
the yard to thirsty plants without spilling — first with a small cup, then a
big cup, then the wobble route around obstacles, then the two-hands teapot
special. Careful beats fast, and the plants "review" each delivery.
**Learning:** carrying control, walking with focus, force/speed regulation,
service pride.
**Modes:**
1. `delivery` "Water Delivery" (type steps): 1 set up: water source on one
   side, thirsty plant on the other (emoji:🪴) → 2 small-cup delivery, slow
   and steady, 60s (emoji:🥤) → 3 big-cup delivery, 60s (emoji:🥛) →
   4 wobble route: around a chair and over the tape river, 90s (emoji:🌀) →
   5 the plant leaves a five-star review (emoji:⭐).
2. `express` "Express Round" (type steps): three deliveries back-to-back with
   the fill-carry-pour-return cycle, 90s each — spills mean a friendly
   "cleanup pit stop" not a fail (emoji:🧻) — ends with Delivery Star badge
   (emoji:🏅).
**Voice tone:** delivery-dispatch playful; plants have opinions and gratitude.
**Assets needed:** art — cups, watering can, thirsty/happy plant faces,
delivery badge; voice — all config lines.
