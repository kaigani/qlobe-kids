### Mini-GDD: Melting Race (games/melting-race/)

**Category:** sensorial-science · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🧊
**Premise:** four ice cubes, four fates: sun, shade, a warm hand, and a cozy
cloth wrap. The tablet coaches the setup, runs check-in timers, and asks the
child to CALL the race out loud ("Sunny is winning!"). Real melting, real
waiting, real science.
**Learning:** heat and melting cause-and-effect, prediction, patient
observation, comparing conditions.
**Modes:**
1. `race` "The Big Melt" (type steps): 1 get four ice cubes on four little
   plates (emoji:🧊) → 2 one in the sun, one in shade, hold one, wrap one,
   60s setup (emoji:📍) → 3 predict the winner OUT LOUD (emoji:🗣️) →
   4 first check-in, 2min timer (emoji:🔍, ask which is smallest?) →
   5 final check + crown the winner (emoji:🏆).
2. `rescue` "Ice Rescue" (type steps): freeze a tiny toy in a cup of ice the
   night before (emoji:🥶) → then coach the rescue: warm hands, 60s
   (emoji:🤲) → warm water drips, 60s (emoji:💧) → victory when the toy pops
   free (emoji:🎉).
**Voice tone:** sports announcer meets scientist; the waiting IS the drama.
**Assets needed:** art — ice cubes at melting stages, sun/shade split scene,
frozen toy; voice — all config lines.
