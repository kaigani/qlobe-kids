### Mini-GDD: Shelf Reset Game (games/shelf-reset-game/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🧺
**Premise:** the Montessori work cycle as a game: choose one tray of toys or
work, use it with full attention, then reset it EXACTLY as found — ready for
the next person. The reset is the win condition, not the play.
**Learning:** work-cycle habit (choose → use → return), order, respect for
shared spaces, finishing what you start.
**Modes:**
1. `cycle` "One Full Cycle" (type steps): 1 walk the shelf and choose ONE tray
   (emoji:🧺) → 2 carry it with two hands to your spot (emoji:🙌) → 3 use it
   with your whole attention, 3min (emoji:🧩) → 4 put every piece back on the
   tray, 60s (emoji:🔄) → 5 return the tray to its exact shelf home
   (emoji:🏠, say the next friend will find it perfect).
2. `inspector` "Shelf Inspector" (type steps): 1 pick one messy shelf spot
   (emoji:🔍) → 2 sort what belongs and what wandered, 60s (emoji:🤔) →
   3 walk the wanderers home, 60s (emoji:🚶) → 4 stand back and admire
   (emoji:✨).
**Voice tone:** serene, unhurried, quietly proud; whisper-level celebration.
**Assets needed:** art — wooden trays, low shelf, work rug; voice — all
config lines.
