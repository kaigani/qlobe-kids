### Mini-GDD: Number Rod Race (games/number-rod-race/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** sequence-order · **splashEmoji:** 📏
**Premise:** Montessori number rods: order them shortest → longest. Rods are
emoji-run cards (`emoji:🟥`, `emoji:🟥🟥`, `emoji:🟥🟥🟥`…) so length is VISIBLE.
**Learning:** length comparison, ordering, 1–5 quantity intuition.
**Modes:** 1. `short` "Short to Long" (rounds 4, difficultyRamp; sets of rods
length 1–5, say lines count the blocks: "One! Two! Three! Short to long!").
2. `long` "Long to Short" (rounds 3; reversed order sets; say lines emphasize
longest-first).
**Assets needed:** proper number-rod art (red/blue segmented rods); voice lines.
