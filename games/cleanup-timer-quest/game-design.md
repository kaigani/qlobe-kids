### Mini-GDD: Cleanup Timer Quest (games/cleanup-timer-quest/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** ⏱️
**Premise:** the classic beat-the-song cleanup, structured: pick the zone,
sort into three piles (toys, clothes, books), then race the friendly timer to
fly each pile home. Fast but never frantic — the timer cheers, it does not
threaten.
**Learning:** sorting-then-storing strategy, time awareness, making chores
feel finishable.
**Modes:**
1. `room` "Room Rescue" (type steps): 1 pick your messy zone (emoji:🗺️) →
   2 make three piles: toys, clothes, books, 90s (emoji:🗂️) → 3 toys fly to
   their bin, 60s (emoji:🧸) → 4 clothes to the basket, 45s (emoji:👕) →
   5 books to the shelf, 45s (emoji:📚) → 6 victory spin in the clean space
   (emoji:🌀).
2. `speed` "Five-Minute Wonder" (type steps): one 5-minute grand timer with
   checkpoint voice pings — halfway cheer, one-minute warning as excitement,
   final countdown as a drumroll (emoji:🥁).
**Voice tone:** game-show host energy with a gentle heart; the room is the
prize.
**Assets needed:** art — toy bin, laundry basket, bookshelf, sparkle-clean
room; voice — all config lines.
