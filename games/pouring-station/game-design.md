### Mini-GDD: Pouring Station (games/pouring-station/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🏺
**Premise:** the tablet coaches real pouring practice — a Montessori classic. The
child gets real cups and a pitcher; the tablet walks them through slow, careful
pouring, one step at a time, and celebrates the practice (spills included — wiping
is part of the work!).
**Learning:** hand control, sequencing, independence, cleanup as part of the task.
**Modes:**
1. `water` "Water Pouring" (type steps): 1 gather pitcher + 2 cups (emoji:🥤) →
   2 fill pitcher a little (emoji:🚰) → 3 pour slooowly into cup one, 30s timer
   (emoji:🥛, after-line praises slow pouring) → 4 pour back the other way, 30s
   (emoji:🏺) → 5 wipe any drips (emoji:🧻, say wiping makes you a pouring pro).
2. `beans` "Bean Pouring" (type steps): same shape with dry beans (emoji:🫘) —
   say beans are great for first-time pourers; listen to the sound they make.
**Voice tone:** calm, unhurried; praise the care, not the result.
**Assets needed (put in ASSETS.md):** art — pitcher, two cups, water stream, beans,
cloth (toy-3D style); voice — all config lines.
