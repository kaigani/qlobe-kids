# Land & Water Tray - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Land & Water Tray (games/land-water-tray/)
**Category:** culture-geography · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🏝️
**Premise:** shape the world by placing land and water pieces onto a tray ghost. Kids make an island, a lake, a peninsula, and a bay, then hear a simple definition for each landform.
**Learning:** landform concepts and land/water opposites.
**Modes:** 1. `forms` "Make the Landform" (rounds 4): island, lake, peninsula, and bay using 5 placeholder land/water tile parts on a tray ghost.
**Assets needed:** sculpted land/water tray art; recorded definition lines.
