# Loose Parts Collage - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Loose Parts Collage (games/loose-parts-collage/)
**Category:** art-music · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🍂
**Premise:** arrange leaves, buttons, and yarn onto the ghost to make nature
art - a sun with leaf rays, a button flower, a yarn rainbow, and a pinecone owl.
The cheer sends kids hunting real loose parts.
**Learning:** composition from found objects, pattern-in-art.
**Modes:** 1. `nature` "Nature Pictures" (rounds 4): sun, button flower,
yarn rainbow, and pinecone owl.
**Assets needed:** photographed-style loose-part art; voice lines.
