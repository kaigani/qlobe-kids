### Mini-GDD: Subtraction Picnic (games/subtraction-picnic/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** tap-count · **splashEmoji:** 🐿️
**Premise:** the picnic guests are hungry! A cheeky squirrel (and friends) eat
some of the snacks. Feed them and count what's left. Taking-away is friendly.
**Learning:** informal subtraction within 6, "how many left?" reasoning.
**Modes:** 1. `picnic` "Hungry Guests" (type takeaway, rounds 5, difficultyRamp):
start 3 apples and squirrel eats 1; start 4 strawberries and bunny eats 2;
start 5 crackers and bird eats 2; start 6 grapes and bear eats 3; start 6
bananas and squirrel eats 4. Food art refs use `shared:foods/apple.png`,
`shared:foods/strawberry.png`, `shared:foods/crackers.png`,
`shared:foods/grapes.png`, and `shared:foods/banana.png`. Guest art is
temporary emoji placeholder art.
**Assets needed:** hungry-guest character art (squirrel/bunny/bird/bear, cast
style); recorded story and count lines.
