### Mini-GDD: Big Paper Murals (games/big-paper-murals/)

**Category:** art-music · **World:** paper-garden · **Engine:** coach-timer ·
**splashEmoji:** 🎨
**Premise:** roll a BIG paper across the floor — the tablet conducts a mural!
Timed drawing movements ("draw giant loops with your WHOLE arm"), theme
prompts, a swap-spots round for siblings/friends, and a gallery-opening
finale where everyone admires the work.
**Learning:** gross-motor drawing (shoulder strokes before finger strokes),
collaboration, creative confidence, sharing space.
**Modes:**
1. `solo` "My Mural" (type steps): 1 roll out big paper + tape corners
   (emoji:📜) → 2 giant arm circles warm-up, 20s (emoji:💪) → 3 draw huge
   loops with your whole arm, 60s (emoji:➰) → 4 fill one corner with tiny
   details, 60s (emoji:🔍) → 5 sign your masterpiece BIG (emoji:✍️).
2. `together` "Mural Party" (type steps): 1 everyone picks a corner
   (emoji:🧑‍🤝‍🧑) → 2 draw in your corner, 90s (emoji:🖍️) → 3 SWAP SPOTS,
   add to a friend's part kindly, 90s (emoji:🔄) → 4 connect all corners
   with one long road or river, 60s (emoji:🛤️) → 5 gallery opening: walk
   the mural and say your favorite part (emoji:🖼️).
**Voice tone:** art-teacher exuberant; big movements get big cheers.
**Assets needed:** art — paper roll, crayon pack, mural-in-progress; voice —
all config lines.
