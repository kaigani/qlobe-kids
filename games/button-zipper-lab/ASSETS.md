# Button-Zipper Lab Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used. The Storybook Rooms background at `assets/bg.jpg` is intentionally not included; the reviewer supplies it.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Big-button shirt: a laid-flat shirt with oversized buttons and clear buttonholes.
- Zipper jacket: a front-facing jacket with both zipper halves easy to distinguish.
- Zipper-car closeup: a clear close view of the zipper slider clicking onto its track.

Voice lines:

- Welcome to the getting-dressed laboratory! Slow hands make excellent experiments.
- Excellent experiment. Take your time.
- Laboratory victory! You practiced every motion with patient hands.
- Button experiment complete. You did a solo victory button!
- Experiment number one! Find a shirt with big buttons and lay it in front of you.
- Experiment number two! Push one button halfway through the door. Go slowly.
- Halfway through the door. The experiment is working!
- Pinch the button and pull it the rest of the way through. Patient fingers can do it.
- Push, pinch, pull. One button is through!
- Now do the whole row slowly, one button at a time. Rest your hands whenever they need it.
- The whole row is buttoned. Careful science!
- Solo victory button! Choose one button and do the whole push, pinch, and pull by yourself.
- Zipper experiment complete. Your zipper car made the full trip!
- Experiment number one! Find a jacket and place it in front of you.
- Experiment number two! Click the zipper car onto its track. The little car needs its track, so take your time.
- Click! The zipper car found its track.
- Hold the bottom steady and zip up slowly.
- The zipper car traveled all the way up!
- Now guide the zipper car back down slowly.
- Final experiment! Click in, hold steady, and do one full zip at your real speed.
