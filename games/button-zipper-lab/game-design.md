### Mini-GDD: Button-Zipper Lab (games/button-zipper-lab/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🧥
**Premise:** the getting-dressed laboratory! With a real jacket or shirt in
hand, the tablet coaches buttons and zippers one motion at a time — slow
motion first, then "for real speed", then the victory lap of doing it solo.
**Learning:** buttoning, zipping, fine-motor persistence, dressing
independence.
**Modes:**
1. `buttons` "Button Lab" (type steps): 1 find a shirt with big buttons
   (emoji:👕) → 2 push one button halfway through, 30s (emoji:🔘, say push
   the button through the door) → 3 pull it the rest of the way, 30s
   (emoji:🤏) → 4 do the whole row slowly, 90s (emoji:🧵) → 5 solo victory
   button! (emoji:🏅).
2. `zippers` "Zipper Lab" (type steps): 1 find a jacket (emoji:🧥) → 2 click
   the zipper car onto its track, 30s (emoji:🚂, say the little car needs its
   track) → 3 zip up slowly, 20s (emoji:⬆️) → 4 zip down (emoji:⬇️) →
   5 full zip at real speed (emoji:🏎️).
**Voice tone:** lab-scientist playful ("experiment number two!"); patience
built into every line.
**Assets needed:** art — big-button shirt, zipper jacket, zipper-car closeup;
voice — all config lines.
