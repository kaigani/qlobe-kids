# Silly Sentence Builder - Game Design

### Mini-GDD: Silly Sentence Builder (games/silly-sentence-builder/)
**Category:** oral-storytelling · **Engine:** build-assemble · **splashEmoji:** 🤪
**Premise:** build sentences from WHO + DOES + WHERE cards, and they come out
gloriously silly. On completion the voice reads the whole sentence with drama
("The penguin dances on the moon!"). Every build is correct structurally;
silliness is the reward.
**Learning:** sentence structure (subject-verb-place), oral rehearsal.
**Modes:** 1. `silly` "Make a Silly Sentence" (rounds 4, ordered): each build =
3 slots (who/does/where); emoji picture cards make a target sentence with ghost
hints, then say the full silly sentence. 2. `sillier` "Even Sillier" (rounds 3):
4 slots add a HOW card (slowly / super fast / sneakily).
**Assets needed:** word-card art per part; recorded dramatic sentence readings.
