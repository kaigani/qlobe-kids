### Mini-GDD: Pattern Train (games/pattern-train/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** pattern-continue · **splashEmoji:** 🚂
**Premise:** the pattern train needs its next cars. Color and shape wagons repeat,
and the child taps the wagon that keeps the train rolling.
**Learning:** AB/ABC/AAB pattern recognition and extension.
**Modes:** 1. `colors` "Color Cars" (rounds 5, difficultyRamp): red, blue, and
yellow wagon placeholders with AB, ABC, and AAB patterns, including one- and
two-car endings. 2. `shapes` "Shape Cars" (rounds 4): triangle, circle, and
square wagon placeholders with spoken shape names.
**Assets needed:** train-car art per color/shape; recorded voice lines.
