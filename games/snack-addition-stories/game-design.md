### Mini-GDD: Snack Addition Stories (games/snack-addition-stories/)
**Category:** math-number-sense · **Engine:** tap-count · **splashEmoji:** 🍓
**Premise:** little snack stories that add up. "Maya has TWO strawberries...
and ONE more! How many?" The child collects the total while the voice tells the
story; the count-aloud answers the story's question.
**Learning:** informal addition to 6, number stories.
**Modes:** 1. `stories` "Snack Stories" (type collect, rounds 5, difficultyRamp):
each round's say line is an addition story and count = the sum: 1+1 grapes, 2+1
strawberries, 2+2 crackers, 3+1 apples, 3+3 bananas. Food art refs use
`shared:foods/grapes.png`, `shared:foods/strawberry.png`,
`shared:foods/crackers.png`, `shared:foods/apple.png`, and
`shared:foods/banana.png`. Cast names rotate in the stories (Maya, Leo, Nia,
Sam, Ravi).
**Assets needed:** none new for art; recorded story and count lines.
