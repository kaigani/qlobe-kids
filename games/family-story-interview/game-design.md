### Mini-GDD: Family Story Interview (games/family-story-interview/)

**Category:** oral-storytelling · **World:** story-screen · **Engine:**
observe-journal · **splashEmoji:** 🎙️
**Premise:** the child becomes a reporter interviewing a grown-up about the
long-ago days. Each page is one interview question the tablet asks ALOUD for
the child to repeat ("What games did you play when you were five?"); after
listening, the child stamps stickers for what they learned. The recap replays
the whole interview as a story.
**Learning:** asking questions, listening and retelling, family connection,
past-vs-now vocabulary.
**Modes:**
1. `childhood` "When You Were Five" (3 pages): questions about games
   (emoji:🪀), food (emoji:🍲), school (emoji:🎒); stickers: same-as-me 🤝,
   different 🔀, surprising 😲, funny 😂.
2. `places` "Where You Lived" (2 pages): questions about home (emoji:🏡) and
   journeys (emoji:🚂).
**Voice tone:** newsroom-playful ("This just in!"); models each question
slowly so the child can re-ask it.
**Assets needed:** art — microphone, reporter notebook, era props (rotary
phone, cassette); voice — all config lines.
