### Mini-GDD: Throwing Target Garden (games/throwing-target-garden/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🎯
**Premise:** set up a target garden — buckets, hoops, chalk circles — and the
tablet runs a friendly throwing clinic: underhand lobs, step-and-throw,
distance ladders, and a wacky trick-shot finale. Beanbags, rolled socks,
soft balls all work.
**Learning:** throwing mechanics (underhand first), aim and force control,
turn-taking, celebrating near-misses.
**Modes:**
1. `clinic` "Throwing Clinic" (type steps): 1 build three targets — near,
   middle, far (emoji:🪣) → 2 underhand lobs at NEAR, 45s (emoji:🫳) →
   3 step-with-the-other-foot throws at MIDDLE, 45s (emoji:🦶) → 4 big
   rainbow throws at FAR, 45s (emoji:🌈) → 5 pick your best throw and show
   it off (emoji:🏆).
2. `wacky` "Trick Shots" (type steps): backwards-over-the-shoulder (emoji:🙃),
   under-the-leg (emoji:🦵), eyes-closed-after-aiming (emoji:😴), invent
   your own trick shot and NAME it (emoji:✨).
**Voice tone:** sports-clinic upbeat; near-misses get "SO close!" energy.
**Assets needed:** art — beanbags, bucket targets, chalk circles; voice —
all config lines.
