# Throwing Target Garden Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used. The configured Field Journal background path is reserved for the reviewer-supplied `assets/bg.jpg` and is not included here.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Beanbags: soft, colorful throwing beanbags suitable for young children.
- Bucket targets: near, middle, and far buckets with friendly garden styling.
- Chalk circles: large outdoor chalk target circles.
- Field Journal background: warm hand-crafted outdoor target-garden scene for `assets/bg.jpg`.

Voice lines:

- Welcome to the target garden! Grab beanbags, rolled socks, or soft balls.
- Great throw! Near-miss? You were SO close!
- Target garden training complete! What fantastic throwing!
- Clinic complete! You practiced aim, force, and strong throwing form.
- Build three safe targets: one near, one in the middle, and one far away.
- Underhand lobs at the near target. Swing low and let go gently.
- Nice underhand practice! Hits and near-misses both build your aim.
- Throw at the middle target. Step with the foot opposite your throwing hand.
- Strong step-and-throw practice! You were SO close!
- Make big rainbow throws at the far target. Aim high and choose your force.
- Those throws made giant rainbow shapes!
- Pick your best throw and show it off like a champion.
- What a wacky shot! Near-miss? You were SO close!
- Trick-shot champion! Your brand-new throw deserves a name.
- Try a backwards, over-the-shoulder trick shot. Check that the space behind you is clear.
- Try an under-the-leg trick shot. Keep your throw soft and safe.
- Aim first, close your eyes, then make a gentle throw.
- Invent your own trick shot and give it an amazing name!
