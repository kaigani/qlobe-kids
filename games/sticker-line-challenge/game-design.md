### Mini-GDD: Sticker Line Challenge (games/sticker-line-challenge/)

**Category:** writing-fine-motor · **Engine:** trace-path · **splashEmoji:** ⭐
**Premise:** a craft table full of sticker paths! The child traces along dotted
curves, zigzags, and loops — every checkpoint they pass "sticks" a sticker on
the line. Finish the path and the whole sticker chain wiggles with joy. This is
pre-writing stroke practice dressed up as decorating.
**Learning:** controlled line-following left→right, curve and zigzag strokes
(pre-writing shapes), pencil-grip-adjacent finger control.
**Modes:**
1. `waves` "Wavy Lines" (3 rounds): gentle horizontal wave paths, growing
   amplitude. Prompt lines like "Trace the wavy line. Stick a star on every dot!"
2. `zigzags` "Zigzag Trails" (3 rounds): sharp zigzag paths, growing count of
   turns. Praise crisp corners ("What a pointy turn!").
3. `loops` "Loop-the-Loops" (3 rounds): big loops and circles. Say loops are
   how letters like l and e begin.
**Voice tone:** crafty, encouraging, celebrates neatness gently but never
punishes wobbles.
**Assets needed:** art — star/heart/flower sticker sheets, craft-table props;
voice — all config lines.
