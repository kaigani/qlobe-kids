### Mini-GDD: Scissor Trail Safari (games/scissor-trail-safari/)

**Category:** writing-fine-motor · **Engine:** trace-path · **splashEmoji:** ✂️
**Premise:** a paper safari! Dotted cutting lines wander past paper-craft
animals. The child "cuts" along the trail with their finger-scissors — the
paper parts on either side as they go. Reaching the animal at the end sets it
free with a happy hop. (Pairs with real scissor practice: the ASSETS/GDD note
invites doing the same lines with real paper and child scissors.)
**Learning:** scissor-line control, smooth continuous strokes, hand-eye
steering along a path.
**Modes:**
1. `straight` "Straight Cuts" (3 rounds): straight and gently angled lines to
   reach the lion, zebra, giraffe (emoji:🦁 🦓 🦒).
2. `curvy` "Curvy Cuts" (3 rounds): S-curves and arcs to reach the parrot,
   snake, monkey (emoji:🦜 🐍 🐵).
3. `spiral` "Spiral Rescue" (2 rounds): one big spiral each to reach the
   elephant and tiger (emoji:🐘 🐯) — the trickiest cut of all.
**Voice tone:** safari-guide playful; "steady scissors" language; celebrate
each freed animal by name.
**Assets needed:** art — paper-craft safari animals, dotted cut lines, paper
texture; voice — all config lines.
