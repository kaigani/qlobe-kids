# Scissor Trail Safari assets

Current art is Unicode emoji rendered by the shared engine on soft rounded
cards. No image or recorded-audio files are included. The configured Paper
Garden background at `./assets/bg.jpg` is a production placeholder supplied by
the reviewer. Voice uses the local Web Speech wrapper in `shared/js/speech.js`.

For an offline companion activity, an adult can copy these trails onto real
paper and invite the child to follow them with child-safe scissors and direct
supervision.

## Assets needed

### Art

- Paper-craft lion, zebra, and giraffe — friendly straight-trail rescue targets.
- Paper-craft parrot, snake, and monkey — playful curved-trail rescue targets.
- Paper-craft elephant and tiger — celebratory spiral rescue targets.
- Dotted cut lines — bold, high-contrast paper cutting guides with rounded dots.
- Paper texture — softly fibrous layered-paper surface behind the cutting trails.
- Paper Garden background — tactile paper safari scene for `./assets/bg.jpg`.
