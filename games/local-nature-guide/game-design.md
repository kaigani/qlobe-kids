### Mini-GDD: Local Nature Guide (games/local-nature-guide/)

**Category:** culture-geography · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🌲
**Premise:** the child trains as the family's nature guide: collect and study
real finds — a pinecone, a special rock, a leaf — and practice a guide's
introduction for each ("This is a pinecone. It comes from a pine tree. Feel
how bumpy!"). Ends with a guided tour for a grown-up.
**Learning:** local-nature knowledge, descriptive language, teaching-back
confidence, respectful collecting.
**Modes:**
1. `collect` "Guide Training" (type steps): 1 find one pinecone or seed thing
   (emoji:🌰) → 2 find one special rock, 90s (emoji:🪨) → 3 find one
   interesting leaf (emoji:🍁) → 4 study each: feel, smell, look close, 60s
   (emoji:🔍) → 5 practice your guide sentence for each, 90s (emoji:🗣️).
2. `tour` "Give the Tour" (type steps): 1 arrange your museum on a cloth
   (emoji:🧺) → 2 invite your visitor (emoji:🎟️) → 3 present each treasure
   with your guide sentence, 2min (emoji:🎤) → 4 answer one visitor question
   (emoji:❓) → 5 guide badge ceremony (emoji:🏅).
**Voice tone:** park-ranger warm; treats every pebble as museum-grade.
**Assets needed:** art — pinecone, rocks, leaves, nature-museum cloth, guide
badge; voice — all config lines.
