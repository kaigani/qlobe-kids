# Sweep the Trail Assets

All current art is emoji placeholder art rendered by the `coach-timer` engine on soft rounded cards. No image files, generated art, downloaded art, recorded clips, or network assets are used. The Storybook Rooms background at `assets/bg.jpg` is intentionally not included; the reviewer supplies it.

## Assets needed

Art:

- Broom: a child-sized broom with a broad, friendly silhouette.
- Dustpan: a sturdy dustpan shown receiving a small treasure pile.
- Leaf pile: a soft, colorful pile of dry leaves on a clear path.
- Tape square: a highly visible landing-zone square marked on the floor.

Voice lines:

- Trail ranger, grab your broom. We are sweeping hidden treasure into one grand pile!
- Great trail work, ranger.
- Trail cleared! You cared for this space from edge to finish.
- Crumb treasure collected. Look at that clean floor!
- With a grown-up, make a tape square on the floor. That is our treasure landing zone.
- Sweep the edges toward the middle with steady strokes.
- The treasure pile is growing in the middle!
- Corners hide crumbs! Use little strokes to guide them out.
- You found the hidden corner crumbs.
- Hold the dustpan still and sweep the treasure pile inside.
- Step back and admire the clean floor. Trail cleared!
- Leaf treasure collected. The path looks cared for!
- With a grown-up, mark a landing zone on the path for our leaf treasure.
- Sweep the leaves from the path edges toward the landing zone.
- The leaf treasure pile is growing!
- Corners hide little leaves! Use short strokes to bring them to the pile.
- You found the hidden corner leaves.
- Bonus step! Ask your grown-up, then jump into the leaf pile and hop back out.
- Gather the leaf treasure with the dustpan or your grown-up’s leaf container.
- Step back and admire the clear path. Trail cleared!
