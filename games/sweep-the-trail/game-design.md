### Mini-GDD: Sweep the Trail (games/sweep-the-trail/)

**Category:** practical-life · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🧹
**Premise:** real sweeping, gamified gently: mark a "landing zone" with tape,
then the tablet coaches sweeping crumbs or leaves into it — small strokes,
corners, the grand final pile, and the dustpan finish.
**Learning:** whole-arm coordination, thorough-vs-quick work habits, caring
for spaces.
**Modes:**
1. `indoor` "Crumb Patrol" (type steps): 1 make a tape square on the floor
   (emoji:📦) → 2 sweep the edges toward the middle, 60s (emoji:🧹) →
   3 little strokes for the corners, 30s (emoji:🪥, say corners hide crumbs!)
   → 4 dustpan scoop (emoji:🗑️) → 5 admire the clean floor (emoji:✨).
2. `outdoor` "Leaf Trail" (type steps): same shape with leaves on a path
   (emoji:🍂), plus a jump-in-the-pile bonus step (emoji:🤸) before the scoop.
**Voice tone:** trail-ranger cheerful; the pile is treasure.
**Assets needed:** art — broom, dustpan, leaf pile, tape square; voice — all
config lines.
