### Mini-GDD: Nature Scavenger Hunt (games/nature-scavenger-hunt/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🔎
**Premise:** a sensory scavenger hunt by QUALITY, not by name: find something
smooth, something green, something round, something tiny, something that
makes a sound. Timed missions, show-and-tell moments between finds.
**Learning:** sensory vocabulary (texture/color/shape/size), outdoor
attention, categorising the real world.
**Modes:**
1. `classic` "Five Finds" (type steps): find something smooth, 90s (emoji:🪨)
   → something green (emoji:🍃) → something round (emoji:⚪) → something
   tiny (emoji:🐜) → something that makes a sound when you shake it
   (emoji:🎶); after each: hold it up and SAY why it counts.
2. `texture` "Feely Hunt" (type steps): something bumpy (emoji:🌰), something
   soft (emoji:🪶), something cold (emoji:🧊-like), something tickly
   (emoji:🌾) — eyes-closed feel test at the end.
**Voice tone:** treasure-hunter hushed excitement; "why it counts" is
celebrated over correctness.
**Assets needed:** art — magnifying glass, find-cards for each quality;
voice — all config lines.
