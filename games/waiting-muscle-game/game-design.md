### Mini-GDD: Waiting Muscle Game (games/waiting-muscle-game/)

**Category:** social-emotional · **World:** story-screen · **Engine:**
coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 💪
**Premise:** the marshmallow test as a friendly workout: put a small treat or
favorite toy in front of you, and train your "waiting muscle" — 10 seconds,
then 20, then a whole minute — with coached waiting TRICKS (sit on hands,
sing inside your head, look away and count). The wait ends in the treat AND
the bigger win: "you just got stronger."
**Learning:** delayed gratification, self-regulation strategies, naming and
celebrating self-control.
**Modes:**
1. `train` "Waiting Workout" (type steps): 1 place the treat where you can
   see it (emoji:🍪) → 2 ten-second wait, hands on knees, 10s (emoji:🙌) →
   3 twenty seconds using the look-away trick, 20s (emoji:👀) → 4 thirty
   seconds singing inside your head, 30s (emoji:🎵) → 5 EAT or PLAY —
   you earned it, champion (emoji:🏆).
2. `master` "Waiting Master" (type steps): the one-minute grand wait with
   coached check-ins every 15s ("your muscle is GROWING") → then the
   double-treat rule discussion: wait again to make it two? Child chooses —
   both choices are celebrated (emoji:🤔 → emoji:🎉).
**Voice tone:** gym-coach gentle; waiting is portrayed as literal strength
training with visible progress.
**Assets needed:** art — waiting-muscle mascot arm, treat plate, trick
icons; voice — all config lines.
