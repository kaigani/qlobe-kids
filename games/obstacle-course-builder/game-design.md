### Mini-GDD: Obstacle Course Builder (games/obstacle-course-builder/)

**Category:** movement-outdoor · **Engine:** coach-timer · **splashEmoji:** 🏗️
**Premise:** design-then-run! First the tablet coaches BUILDING the course
from household stuff (cushion mountain, chair tunnel, tape river, sock-ball
carry), then it runs the course with the child — station by station — and
ends with a slow-motion victory replay run.
**Learning:** planning-then-doing, varied gross-motor patterns (crawl, hop,
balance, carry), safe risk play, course design creativity.
**Modes:**
1. `build` "Build It" (type steps): 1 cushion mountain to climb over
   (emoji:⛰️) → 2 chair tunnel to crawl under, 60s (emoji:⛺) → 3 tape
   river to jump across (emoji:🌊) → 4 sock-ball carry zone: spoon or cup
   (emoji:🥄) → 5 finish-line flag or ribbon (emoji:🏁).
2. `run` "Run It" (type steps): full course run, one station per step with
   its own timer — climb 30s, crawl 30s, jump 20s, carry 40s — then the
   slow-motion champion replay run where the tablet narrates like a nature
   documentary (emoji:🎬).
**Voice tone:** construction-foreman turned race-announcer; safety checks
sound like pro protocols.
**Assets needed:** art — cushion mountain, chair tunnel, tape river, finish
flag; voice — all config lines.
